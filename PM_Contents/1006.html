Cleaning/Replacement of Auto Drain Strainer

Maintenance period: Every 500 Hours

WARNING

•Be sure to turn OFF the power supply and confirm that the Air Dryer has completely stopped performing this procedure.

•Wear safety gloves and safety glasses when performing this procedure.

•Do not remove the case assembly with air pressure remaining inside under any circumstances. Air pressure can shoot the casting at high speed when unscrewed, which may result in serious injury or death.

CAUTION

•The parts inside may still be hot even after operation is stopped. Wait (for about 10 - 15 minutes) to allow the parts to cool down before starting work.

Procedure (Refer to Figure 11.3)

1)Turn OFF the power supply.

2)Turn OFF the incoming air supply to the incoming piping [3].

3)Loosen the screws, and remove the front cover [2].

4)Place an oil pan beneath the auto drain [14].

5)Remove the drain tube [11] while pressing up on the release bush [10].

6)Open the residual pressure release clock [9] on the drain port knob [12], and release the air pressure remaining inside the device by shutting OFF outgoing piping [4].

CAUTION

Drain liquid may splash out due to the remaining air pressure inside.

7)Remove the auto drain [14] after confirming the liquid is completely drained out.

a)Gently grip the auto drain [14], and pull down the lock button [8] with your thumb, and then rotate the case assembly 45° CW or CCW to align the match marks [13].

b)Release the lock button [8] and pull the auto drain [14] slowly down to remove it.

8)Remove the auto drain strainer [5] from the inside the case [7], and wash it.

9)Replace the auto drain strainer [5] if it is very dirty.

10)Pour neutral detergent mixed with water into the case [7] and shake well to clean it.

11)Check for damage on the case [7] and for any cracks and dirt on the O ring [6]. If it is very dirty or has cracks or damage, replace it.

a)Apply grease thinly to the O ring [6] before fitting it back into the case [7] groove.

12)Install the cleaned auto drain strainer [5] in the case [7] and attach the auto drain [14] back to the air dryer [1] by aligning it with the match mark [13] and rotating it till it locks.

13)Tighten the residual pressure release cock [9], and reconnect the drain tube [11] and the front cover [2].

14)Supply air to the incoming piping [3].

This completes the auto drain strainer cleaning procedure.
