Checking of Cyclone Filter Inlet Pressure

Maintenance period: Daily

WARNING

•When performing any work, be sure to place a warning tag on each operation panel, main power switch and other locations to ensure that other personnel do not inadvertently operate the machine (Refer to Chapter 1 Safety "1.6 Implementing Lock-out and Tag-out").

Procedure (Refer to Figure 10.4)

1)Turn ON the power supply.

2)Turn ON the Nozzle Coolant by M-code (M08) or Main Operator Panel Switch.

3)Confirm the pressure gauge [2] value for the cyclone filter inlet is within the proper range (0.17 ± 0.05MPa).

4)If pressure value is not at the proper level, perform the following procedures:

•Coolant Tank Level Check (Refer to 10.2 Checking/Replenishment of Cutting Fluid Level).

•Coolant Tank and Pump Cleaning (Refer to 10.5 Cleaning of Coolant Tank and 10.6 Cleaning of Coolant Pumps).

This completes the checking of cyclone filter inlet and pressure procedure.
