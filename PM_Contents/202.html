Replacing the Hydraulic Oil

Maintenance period: Every 2000 Hours

WARNING

•Make sure to turn OFF the power supply and confirm that the hydraulic unit has completely stopped before performing this procedure.

•Wear safety gloves and safety glasses when performing this procedure.

•Ensure the hydraulic unit and oil has cooled down sufficiently to avoid burns.

NOTICE

•When using a hydraulic oil that is not specified here, or if changing the hydraulic oil, be sure to flush the hydraulic unit thoroughly with the same type of flushing oil as the hydraulic oil to be used, then replace the hydraulic oil.

Procedure (Refer to Figure 8.2)

1)Turn OFF the power supply.

2)Place an oil pan under the drain port [2].

3)Remove the cap from the oil fill port [1].

4)Remove the drain plug in the drain port [2], and drain all of the hydraulic oil.

5)Wrap sealing tape around the drain plug, and mount the drain plug to the drain port [2].

6)Fill with hydraulic oil until the oil level reached the upper limit line [4] on the oil level gauge [3].

7)Replace the cap on the oil fill port [1].

This completes replacement of the hydraulic oil.
