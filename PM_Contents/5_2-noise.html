Noise

The noise level of the machine is shown below.

•Noise level: 80 dB maximum (A-weighted sound level)

These values are measured values at full operation (maximum rotation of the spindle without machining a workpiece) with the safety guards and covers correctly mounted and all doors closed.

DANGER

•When a workpiece is machined, noise at 80 dB or higher may occur under certain customer cutting conditions. When performing work near the machine during machining, be sure to wear ear protection and other equipment to ensure that your health is not harmed.

Performing work without wearing ear protection may lead to hearing loss.

 

-NOTE-

The above noise level is measured at a location that is a distance of 0.5 m from the machine and a height of 1.2m above the floor.
