Replacing the Mist Separator Filter

Maintenance period: Every 2000 Hours

WARNING

•Turn OFF the machine power supply before performing the following procedures.

•Do not remove the bowl guard while pressure remains inside the mist separator.

Procedure (Refer to Figure 9.3)

1)Turn OFF the power supply.

2)Stop or disconnect the air source (primary air supply) from the factory facilities.

WARNING

Perform the lock-out and tag-out procedures at the air source from the factory facilities.

3)Confirm that the pressure gauge [1] of the main air unit indicates 0 MPa.

4)Remove the bowl guard [5].

5)Remove the bowl [4].

6)Remove the filter element [6].

7)Mount a new filter element [6].

8)Mount the bowl [4].

9)Mount the bowl guard [5].

10)Restart or reconnect the air supply (primary air supply) from the factory facilities.

This completes the replacement of the mist separator filter.
