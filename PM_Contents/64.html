Cleaning the Filter for the Cooling Fan

Maintenance period: Every 500 Hours

The filter is located on the top right side of the Machine Tool Cabinet (MTC).

Procedure (Refer to Figure 12.1)

1)Turn OFF the power supply.

2)Lift the air filter [1] up and pull it outwards.

3)Slide the air filter [1] downwards to remove.

4)Use a vacuum cleaner or some other tool to suck up dust and dirt on the air filter [1]. If the air filter is particularly dirty, wash it with water using a neutral cleanser, and dry it completely.

CAUTION

Use a neutral detergent diluted with water for cleaning. Do not use benzine, thinner or other solvents.

5)Insert the air filter [1] into its original location.

This completes cleaning of the filter for the cooling fan.
