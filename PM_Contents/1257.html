Replacing the Main Air Unit Filter

Maintenance period: Every 2000 Hours

WARNING

•Turn OFF the power supply before performing the following procedures.

•Do not remove the bowl while pressure remains inside the filter regulator.

Procedure (Refer to Figure 9.4)

1)Turn OFF the power supply.

2)Stop or disconnect the air source (primary air supply) from the factory facilities.

WARNING

Perform the lock-out and tag-out procedures at the air source from the factory facilities.

3)Confirm that the pressure gauge [5] of the main air unit indicates 0 Mpa.

4)While holding the latch [2], turn the bowl [1] and pull the bowl out in the downward direction.

5)Remove the filter element [4].

6)Mount a new filter element [4].

7)While pushing up the bowl [1], turn until the alignment mark [3] is at the matching position.

8)Restart or reconnect the air supply (primary air supply) from the factory facilities.

This completes the replacement of the filter regulator element.
