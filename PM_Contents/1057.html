Replacing the Grease Cartridge

Maintenance period: Every 500 Hours

WARNING

•Turn OFF the power supply before performing the following procedures.

•Wear safety gloves and safety glasses when performing this procedure.

Procedure (Refer to Figure 7.1 and 7.2)

1)Turn OFF the power supply.

2)Turn the cover [1] to remove it.

3)Turn the grease cartridge [2] to remove it.

4)Remove the cap and packing from the new grease cartridge.

5)Install the new grease cartridge and replace the cover [1].

NOTICE

For additional details on the replacement procedure, refer to the instructions indicated on the cartridge box.

6)Remove the air discharge screw [3], and turn ON the power supply.

WARNING

Always remove the air cartridge screw before turning ON the power supply. If the air discharge screw is not removed while the power is ON, the grease will be pressurized and may discharge rapidly causing injury.

7)Release the pressure of the automatic grease supply unit.

a)Select [Automatic Grease Supply Unit] [7] on the Device Manager screen.

b)Press the [Cycle Pause] button [4].

8)Discharge the air.

a)Press the [Cycle Pause] button [4] to cancel the cycle pause mode.

b)Press the [Initial Mode] button [5] to discharge the air from the air discharge port.

c)Once the air is no longer discharged from the air discharged port, press the [Initial Mode] button [5] again to stop the pump.

d)Tighten discharge screw [3].

e)Wipe off grease adhering to the area around the air discharged port.

9)Replace the air discharge screw [3] and tighten.

10)Press the [Alarm Reset] button [6].

This completes the replacement of the grease cartridge.
