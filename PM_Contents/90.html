Cleaning the Spindle Taper Section

Maintenance period: Daily

WARNING

•Turn ON the machine power supply, and confirm that all machine movements have stopped prior to performing the following procedures.

•Exercise extreme caution as entry into the machine is necessary in order to perform the following procedures.

Procedure (Refer to Figure 4.1)

1)Turn ON the power.

2)Return the spindle-clamped tool to the tool magazine.

3)Move the spindle to an axis position where it is easy to perform work.

4)Open the operator door, and then turn OFF the power supply.

5)Remove chips and cutting fluid out of the spindle taper section using the dedicated jig [2] or clean cloth.

NOTICE

Do not use compressed air. It may cause damage to sealant sections due to infiltration of chips and cutting fluid into the spindle.

6)Clean the spindle nose with clean cloth.

This completes cleaning of the spindle taper section.
