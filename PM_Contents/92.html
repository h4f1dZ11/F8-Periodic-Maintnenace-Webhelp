Replacing the Lubricant Oil

Maintenance period: Every 4000 Hours

WARNING

•Be sure to turn OFF the power supply and confirm that the lubricant unit has completely stopped before performing this procedure.

•Wear safety gloves and safety glasses when performing this procedure.

Procedure (Refer to Figure 6.1)

1)Turn OFF the power supply.

2)Place the oil pan under the drain port [5].

3)Remove the drain plug in the drain port [5], and drain all of the lubricant.

4)Wrap sealing tape around the drain plug, and mount it to the drain port [5].

5)Remove the fill port cap [1].

6)Refill with lubricant until the oil level reached the upper limit line [3] of the

oil level gauge [2].

7)Attach the fill port cap [1].

This completes replacement of the lubricant.
