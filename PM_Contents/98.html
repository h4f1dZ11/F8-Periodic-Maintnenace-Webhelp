Cleaning the Air Filter and the Condenser

Maintenance period: Every 50 Hours

WARNING

•Be sure to turn OFF the power supply and confirm that the spindle lubricant temperature controller has completely stopped before performing this procedure.

Procedure (Refer to Figure 6.2)

1)Turn OFF the power supply.

2)Remove the air filter [1] by pulling it upward. Once the air filter [1] is removed, the condenser is visible.

3)Clean the air filter [1] in water that does not exceed 40°C, and allow it to fully dry.

NOTICE

Do not use benzene, thinner, or other solvents.

4)Use a brush or some other tool to remove any dirt or dust adhering to the the fins on the condenser.

WARNING

Do not clean the temperature controller by splashing water directly on it. Failure to observe this precaution may result in an electrical shock. This can also cause a breakdown.

CAUTION

Always wear protective gloves during work.

Touching the edges of the fins with your bare hands can result in an injury.

5)Reinstall the air filter [1].

This completes cleaning of the air filter and the condenser.
