Checking of Evaporation Thermometer

Maintenance period: Every 50 Hours

WARNING

•Wear safety gloves and safety glasses when performing this procedure.

Procedure (Refer to Figure 11.1)

1)Confirm that the evaporation thermometer [1] indicator is within the green zone with the power ON.

•If the evaporation thermometer [1] indicator is not within the green zone, ensure the ventilation grill [2] is not obstructed.

•Remove any dirt or dust from the ventilation grill [2] (Refer to 11.2 Cleaning of Ventilation Grill)

This completes the evaporation thermometer checking procedure.
